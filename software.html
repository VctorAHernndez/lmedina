My Software

Risch and Parallel Risch Algorithms

These algorithms are based on Manuel Bronstein's Book: Symbolic Integration I - Transcendental Functions, Algorithms and Computations in Mathematics, Vol.1 Springer, Heidelberg and on Manuel Bronstein's "Poor Man's Integrator". This work was done during the summer and fall 2007 at Wolfram Research Inc. Put all softwares in the same folder. Our Mathematica version of pmint and Manuel Bronstein's original pmint are the main focus of this article by R. Kragler.

Mathematica Packages (supervised by Sasha Pavlyk, WRI)

  2. CoupledDESystem.m
  3. Misc.m
  4. ParametricProblems.m
  5. pmint.m
  6. RationalIntegration.m
  7. RischDE.m
  8. SymbolicIntBook.m
